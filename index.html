Focaccia with Caramelized Onions
Cranberries, and Brie

Wow your taste buds with this festive focaccia appetizer topped with sweet caramelized onions, tart
cranberries, and creamy brie.It will become your new favorite holiday starter!

Image: Sally Vegas

Fresh Cranberry Recipes That Go Beyond Sauce

Ingredients

For the Focaccia

  • 4 cups (500g) all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 teaspons instant yeast
  • 1 1/2 teaspons salt
  • 1 3/4cups (350g) lukewarm water
  • 1/4 cup extra virgin oil, plus more for the pan

For the topping:

  • 3 medium yellow onions, thinly sliced
  • 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar
  • 1-2 tablespoons olive oil, for brushing on top
  • 10 ounces brie, cut into cubes(scrape rind off with a paring knife if desidered,thoug it's not necessary)
  • 1 1/2 cups fresh or frozen cranberries

Method

  1. Make the Focaccia dough

    2. In a medium-sized mixing bowl,or in a stand mixer with a paddle attachment, mix together all the dough ingredients until the flour is fully absorbed.You don't need to actually knea the dough, though I sometimes knead it a few times in the bowl when mixing by hand to incorporate the last of the flour...
